The Best Movies of All Time, According to Abhik

These are my three favourite movies of all time.

Hirak Rajar Deshe

It's a timeless masterpiece. Its brilliance lies in its seamless blend of fantasy, satire, and social commentary.

Titanic

Titanic stands out as a cinematic masterpiece due to its blend of epic romance, historical accuracy, and technical brilliance.

Sholay

It's praised for its iconic characters, memorable dialogue, and impactful story that seamlessly blends action, drama, and comedy.
